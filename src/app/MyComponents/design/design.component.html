Interior design focuses on creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces within a building. It involves selecting and arranging furniture, lighting, colors, and materials to enhance the overall ambiance and functionality of a room. Interior designers consider factors such as layout, flow, and personal style to create a harmonious and inviting environment that reflects the client's preferences and meets their needs.

Exterior design involves the planning and arrangement of outdoor spaces and the exterior appearance of a building or property. It includes elements such as landscaping, architecture, outdoor lighting, and the selection of materials and colors for the facade. Exterior designers aim to create visually appealing and cohesive exteriors that blend with the surroundings, enhance curb appeal, and reflect the desired style or theme of the property.
